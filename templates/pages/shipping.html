Shipping & Delivery

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Delivery Areas

We currently offer nationwide delivery across Ghana. Primary delivery zones include Accra, Tema, Kumasi, and Takoradi. Special arrangements can be made for cross-border logistics within West Africa.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Standard Materials: 2-4 business days within Accra; 5-7 business days for other regions.
  • Custom Mouldings: Production time varies (typically 7-14 days) plus shipping time.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated based on weight, volume, and destination. For large-scale architectural projects, shipping costs will be included in your formal quotation.

4. Receiving Your Order

A representative must be present at the site to receive and sign for materials. Please inspect all boards and profiles for damage before the delivery team departs.

5. Delays

While we maintain a high standard of punctuality, Sellux Plaster Ltd is not responsible for delays caused by roadblocks, heavy traffic, or material shortages from international suppliers.
