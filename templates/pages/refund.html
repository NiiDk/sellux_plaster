Refund and Returns

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Material Returns

Standard materials from our catalogue (e.g., gypsum boards, metal profiles) can be returned within 7 days if the packaging is unopened and the product is in its original condition. A 10% restocking fee may apply.

2. Custom Items

Bespoke cornices or custom-moulded designs are non-refundable once production has commenced, as these are made specifically to your architectural requirements.

3. Service Refunds

Refunds for professional services (installation, skimming) are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you are dissatisfied with the quality of work, our team will perform remedial actions to meet your satisfaction as per the contract.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days via the original payment method (Paystack or Bank Transfer).
