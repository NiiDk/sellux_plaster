General Interior Design

Interior design is a profession that requires specific schooling and formal training. The work involves planning, research, coordination, and management of projects to create functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces. Interior decorating, on the other hand, is the furnishing or adorning of a space with fashionable or beautiful things. It doesn't require formal training.

The timeline of an interior design project can vary greatly depending on the scope of the project. A simple room refresh might take a few weeks, while a full home renovation could take several months. We work closely with our clients to establish a realistic timeline at the start of every project.

There are many popular interior design styles, including Modern, Minimalist, Industrial, Scandinavian, and Bohemian. Each style has its own unique characteristics and aesthetic. We can help you find the style that best suits your taste and lifestyle.

The cost of interior design services can vary widely depending on the scope of the project, the designer's experience, and the location. Some designers charge an hourly rate, while others charge a flat fee. We offer a variety of packages to suit different budgets and needs.
